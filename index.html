FAQ

  • Does WannaCam require an internet connection?
    No. WannaCam works entirely offline. All your data stays on your device.
  • Where are my photos stored?
    Photos are saved to your device's photo library and managed locally via the app's wishlist.
  • Can I recover deleted items?
    Once deleted, items are removed from both the app and your photo library. This action cannot be undone.
  • How do I switch languages?
    WannaCam follows your device's system language. It supports English, Simplified Chinese, and Traditional Chinese.
  • How do I share my wishlist?
    Select items in your wishlist, tap "Generate Image" to create a long-format image, and share it from your Photos library.

Privacy Policy

WannaCam respects your privacy. Here is our commitment to you:

No data collection. WannaCam does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app operates entirely offline.

No analytics or tracking. We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.

No account required. You can use all features without creating an account or providing any personal details.

Local storage only. All photos, wish items, tags, and notes are stored exclusively on your device using Apple's Core Data framework. Nothing leaves your device.

Camera and photo library access. WannaCam requests camera access to capture photos and photo library access to save and manage your wish photos. These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality.

Location access. If you enable the location watermark feature, WannaCam accesses your location only at the moment of capture to stamp the location name on your photo card. Location data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted.
